From left to right:

Top Row - Ibrahim Ziadeh, Sarah Jaber Ziadeh, Fareedeh Ziadeh, Hanna Ziadeh, Yacoub Farhat Ziadeh, and Ziadeh Farhat Ziadeh

Bottom Row - Wardeh Samaan Al Kassis, Said Ibrahim Ziadeh, Ziadeh Farhat Ziadeh, his wife Wardeh Yacoub Ziadeh, Nemeh Elias Farah, Najwa Ziadeh, the two children sitting are Yousef Ibrahim Ziadeh and Julia Yacoub Ziadeh.
